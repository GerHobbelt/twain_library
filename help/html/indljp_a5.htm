Obtaining TWAIN Information

 Top 

Even though DTWAIN simplifies TWAIN greatly, you should have the TWAIN specification.  The specification goes into much detail on aspects of TWAIN, including state transitions, device capabilities, and other useful information.

 

To download a copy of the TWAIN specification, you can go to the Dynarithmic Technologies website at http://www.dynarithmic.com and follow the link to the TWAIN specification (the current version is 1.8)

 

You can also get a hardcopy of the TWAIN specification, as well as sample programs from the following website:
